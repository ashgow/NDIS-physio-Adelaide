What is Physiotherapy?

NDIS physio Adelaide is a branch of medicine that focuses on assessing, diagnosing, and treating disorders that affect the musculoskeletal system. This includes the bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons. It also covers the nervous system, which includes the brain and spinal cord.

Arthritis - Although arthritis is usually associated with old age, it can affect people of all ages. There exist two primary types of arthritis: osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.
Osteoarthritis is the most common type of arthritis. Symptoms include pain, stiffness, and swelling in the joints. The culprit is the wear and tear of the cartilage that cushions the joints. This can happen due to injury, overuse, or simply aging.

What Makes Physiotherapy So Effective?

Physiotherapy is an extremely effective treatment because it's based on the principles of human anatomy and physiology. The human body is a remarkable machine that can heal itself.
It simply provides the necessary support to the body to do what it's designed to do. Therefore, physiotherapy is often referred to as a "hands-on" approach to health care. It means that the physio Adelaide is working with the body, not against it.
Another reason why NDIS physio Adelaide is so effective is that it's based on the latest scientific research. The techniques and exercises that are used have been proven to be effective through rigorous testing.
The physiotherapist doesn't rely on drugs or guesswork. Every aspect of the treatment process is based on solid evidence.
